I was really looking forward to using my iPhone to access my Slingbox when the mobile application came out (and had even convinced myself that the $30 would be worth it), but that's not a concern anymore since Sling has decided that they're arbitrarily removing support for non-pro devices. I miss the days when companies encouraged upgrading by adding features, not by simply deprecating their previous product line.

Dumb move on their part, but it's at least one less thing to waste money on.
